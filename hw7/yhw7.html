My Favorite Story

A summary of a story I love.

The Alchemist — by Paulo Coelho

"And, when you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it."

The Alchemist follows a young shepherd named Santiago who dreams of finding treasure near the Egyptian pyramids. He leaves behind his comfortable life in Spain and sets off on a journey across North Africa in search of his dream.

Along the way he meets many interesting people, including a king, a crystal merchant, and an alchemist who becomes his teacher. Each person teaches him something new about life, courage, and listening to his heart.

The main lesson of the story is that if you truly want something, the universe will help you achieve it — but you have to be brave enough to pursue it. By the end of the journey, Santiago realizes the real treasure was never just gold, but everything he learned and became along the way.
