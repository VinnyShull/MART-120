My Favorite Story: The Tortoise and the Hare

One of my favorite stories is "The Tortoise and the Hare." It is a classic fable about a race between a fast hare and a slow tortoise.

The hare was very confident because he knew he was much faster than the tortoise. During the race, he decided to take a nap since he thought he had plenty of time to win. Meanwhile, the tortoise kept moving slowly but steadily without stopping.

In the end, the tortoise crossed the finish line first while the hare was still sleeping. The story teaches an important lesson: slow and steady wins the race. It reminds us that consistency and hard work matter more than natural talent alone.
